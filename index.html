Kariann is a Graphic Design student at Wake Technical Community College in Raleigh, NC. She will be graduating in December 2021. She is an intern for R&L Triangle Team and does freelance logo and advertisement design on the side. Kariann enjoys drawing with her hands and likes using a drawing tablet connected to her mac in illustrator. About Kariann


Born in Newport News, Virginia, Kariann spent her childhood moving around between Virginia, Texas, and North Carolina. This taught her how to not only be really adaptive but how to also make friends fast and overcome many adversities. Kariann loved art and drawing from a young age. In Middle School Kariann discovered her love for computers. She started at her first fun attempt at coding by creating and modifying her own myspace layouts, and widgets on her page. When myspace died, Kariann went searching for other ways to satisfy her coding itch but it would never work until she was a graphic design student in college. Kariann originally went to ECU for Biology with the idea of being an orthodontist. She did this because she had good grades and was pressured into going to college for something that she could get a job for. Everyone had high expectations of Kariann and had always told her since she was small that the only job you could get as an artist would be an art teacher. Kariann made it through a couple of years at ECU and then decided to take a break from school because she wanted to do something she was passionate about. It was during this break that she met a gentleman in the graphic design program at Pitt Community College. After thorough investigation and research, Kariann decided that she was in love with the idea of being a graphic designer. She spent a semester finishing her prerequisites and applied to Wake Technical Community College. Kariann completed a semester of the graphic design program and then took a break from school for a year and a half. Kariann got married to her wonderful partner and they had a precious baby boy in April 2020. Kariann's life changed forever after becoming a mother, she knew that she needed to finish school and follow her passions so she could not only provide but also inspire her son to overcome adversity and work hard the same way she would. Kariann has been back in the graphic design program for 2 semesters and is working hard towards graduation in Decemebr 2021.
